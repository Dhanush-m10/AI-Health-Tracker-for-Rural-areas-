Project Overview: AI Health Symptom Checker for Rural Areas

1. Mission Statement
The AI Health Symptom Checker is a mission-driven digital health platform designed to bridge the healthcare gap in rural and underserved communities. By leveraging advanced Artificial Intelligence (Gemini 3 Flash), the application provides instant, accessible, and easy-to-understand health guidance for individuals who may not have immediate access to a medical professional.

3. Core Purpose
In many rural areas, the nearest hospital can be hours away. This tool serves as a first-response digital assistant, helping users understand their symptoms, providing immediate home-care precautions, and identifying "red flag" warning signs that require urgent medical attention.

5. Key Features
AI-Powered Symptom Analysis: A natural language interface where users can describe how they feel in simple terms. The AI extracts symptoms and suggests possible common conditions.
Localized Hospital Finder: A dedicated section to help users locate the nearest village health centers, community clinics, and district hospitals, complete with contact information.
Personalized Health Reports: Generates a structured summary including detected symptoms, precautions, and doctor recommendations. Users can download this report to show to a healthcare provider later.
Preventative Health Tips: A curated library of simple, actionable advice on hygiene, clean water, and nutrition to promote long-term wellness in the community.
Emergency Integration: Quick-access buttons for national emergency services (108/102) to ensure critical help is never more than a click away.

7. Design Philosophy (Rural-First UI/UX)
High Accessibility: Large, legible fonts and high-contrast colors for visibility in various lighting conditions.
Icon-Driven Navigation: Uses universal medical and health icons (Lucide) to assist users with varying literacy levels.
Mobile-Optimized: A lightweight, responsive design that performs well on budget smartphones and in areas with fluctuating internet connectivity.
Calm & Trustworthy: A soft blue and white color palette designed to reduce anxiety and build user trust.

9. Technology Stack
Frontend: React 19 (Modern, fast, and scalable).
Styling: Tailwind CSS (Utility-first, responsive design).
AI Engine: Google Gemini 3 Flash (High-speed, intelligent natural language processing).
Animations: Framer Motion (Smooth transitions and interactive feedback).
Icons: Lucide-React (Crisp, professional SVG icons).
10. Important Medical Disclaimer
The application is built with a Safety-First approach. It explicitly states in every section that it is an informational tool only and is not a replacement for professional medical diagnosis, advice, or treatment. It is designed to empower users with knowledge, not to provide a final medical verdict.
